    SAFEGUARDING

    At Exam Armour, we prioritise the safety of every student is our top priority. Every tutor undergoes a DBS check before their first session, and tutoring takes place at our professional venue, Red Gables in Harpenden Collective, where a responsible adult is on standby at all times. Parents and guardians are always welcome to contact us with any concerns @examarmour.com..

    THE HARPENDEN COLLECTIVE

    The Harpenden Collective is a beautifully renovated historic building on West Common, AL5 2JQ, operating as a professional shared workspace and business community in the heart of Harpenden. With a responsible adult on standby at all times and private rooms for every session, parents can trust that their child is in a safe, supervised and professional environment.
